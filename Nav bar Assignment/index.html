OUR MOTTO

Inspire before Expire

OUR MISSION

Enter to Learn Leave to Achieve

Our Intention

Our intention is to mold the young talent in the best way enabling them to face the challenges of the modern era and also equipping them to master the scientific, technological, moral and social aspects of life to prosper and lead ahead in the world and hereafter.

Our Aim

to furnish the institute with quality education . Empowering them to be critical thinkers and great learners, reflecting leadership qualities required to restore the eminence of Muslim Ummah. And education is the way to achieve all these long term goals.

Our Promise

To provide professional learning environment that offers quality education and leadership skills to develop students' intellectual, spiritual, and aesthetic potential and prepare them to become good Pakistani and responsible citizens and contributing members of the society.

Our Learning Culture

Co-curricular refers to activities, programs and learning experiences that complement, in some way, what students are learning in school, experiences that are connected to the academic curriculum.Celebration of Important Days and Festivals are part of our learning culture

TGK Schooling System

(A Project of The Global Knowledge Academy)



The purpose of TGK Schooling System is to prepare students with the promise of enhancing their intellectual,
physical, social, emotional, spiritual, and artistic growth so that they may realize their power for good as citizens of Pakistan


TGKSS aims at providing students with a well-rounded education offering:


A caring environment with a strong academic foundation

An exclusive focus on the individual child

A balance between local and global standards with modern approaches

Individual attention to students
